Welcome to PEFA Church Bukokholo

About Us

PEFA Church Bukokholo is a vibrant community of believers dedicated to spreading the gospel of Jesus Christ. Our vision is to impact lives spiritually, socially, and emotionally through various ministries and outreach programs. Join us as we grow in faith and fellowship.

Sunday School Ministry

We provide children with a strong foundation in Biblical teachings, moral values, and community engagement through fun and interactive lessons.

Hospital Ministry

We extend love, care, and prayers to the sick in hospitals, bringing hope and spiritual support during challenging times.

Prison Ministry

Our mission is to spread the message of hope and redemption to inmates through teachings, counseling, and personal support.

Women Ministry

Empowering women through fellowship, mentorship, and spiritual guidance, enabling them to live purposeful and impactful lives.

Sunday Service Welcome Note

Join us every Sunday for worship, teaching, and fellowship. Experience the power of God's presence and be part of a loving community.
