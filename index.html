Welcome to PEFA Church Bukokholo

About Us

PEFA Church Bukokholo is a community of faith committed to worship, discipleship, and service. Led by Bishop Godfrey Simiyu, we strive to follow God's calling through our ministries, empowering families, and serving society with love and compassion.

Ministries

Sunday School Ministry

We nurture our children in faith, teaching the Word of God with fun and interactive activities.

Hospital Ministry

We visit hospitals offering spiritual support and prayers to the sick.

Prison Ministry

We bring hope and spiritual guidance to inmates through Bible study and counseling.

Women Ministry

Empowering women in faith, leadership, and community service through various activities.
