About Us

PEFA Sirisha District is a vibrant faith community dedicated to spiritual growth, community service, and ministry outreach. Led by Bishop Godfrey Simiyuthen, we strive to impact lives through love, compassion, and empowerment.

Our Ministries

Sunday School Ministry

We nurture the faith of children through engaging Sunday school activities.

Hospital Ministry

We serve the sick and the needy, bringing hope and care to hospitals.

Prison Ministry

We reach out to prisoners offering spiritual guidance and rehabilitation support.

Women Ministry

Empowering women through faith-based activities, support groups, and community service.

Sunday Service Welcome Note

We warmly welcome all visitors and members to our Sunday services. Join us as we worship, pray, and grow together in faith.
