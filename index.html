About Us

PEFA Church Bukokholo is part of PEFA Sirisha Ministry led by Bishop Godfrey Simiyu Simiyuthen. Our journey is centered on faith, love, and service.

Sunday School Ministry

We nurture children in the Word of God through teaching, worship, and fun activities.

Hospital Ministry

We bring hope, prayer, and comfort to the sick and their families.

Prison Ministry

Restoring hope and dignity to inmates through the Gospel.

Women Ministry

Empowering women spiritually, socially, and economically.
